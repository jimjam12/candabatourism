About The Mayor

Mayor Rene Estacio Maglanque

Mayor Rene Estacio Maglanque, widely known as "Ing Malugud," has been a transformative force in Candaba, Pampanga. Graduating from Adamson University with a degree in Civil Engineering, he began his political career as a municipal councilor in 1992, later becoming a Board Member and serving as Assistant Secretary of the Department of Transportation and Communication. Since assuming the mayoral position in 2013, Maglanque has spearheaded initiatives to enhance education, connectivity, and health services in Candaba. Under his leadership, the municipality received recognition for environmental efforts and implemented projects like the Candaba Cut Off Channel to address flood concerns. Maglanque's proactive response to the COVID-19 pandemic included innovative measures like Online Drugstores, a successful Adopt A Family Program, and continuous food distribution, showcasing his commitment to the well-being of Candaba's residents. His administration's positive relationship with the police force has contributed to a relatively low crime rate, fostering a secure environment for potential business growth. As "Ing Malugud," Mayor Maglanque prioritizes inclusivity, ensuring equitable distribution of projects and services among Candaba's thirty-three barangays, solidifying his commitment to the town's unified progress.
